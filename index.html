a brief historie

I am a video artist with a background in experimental film and directing, camera work and editing for TV broadcasts and studio photography and studio lighting (Fashion).

In 2000 I extended my disciplines to VJ work "onstage" video events in combination with 3D designs. During that period I mainly worked on assignment on the visual content translation of visual concepts for art galleries, cultural events, music organizations and "Brands".

In recent years I have started to concentrate more on web 3D programming via Javascript (libraries) and HTML5 and CSS in combination with 2D and 3D design.

Accomplishments

I have worked on national and international presentations for public spaces, live exhibitions for galleries and lectures for the Amsterdamse Grafische Academie and "the Haagse kunst Academie".
In addition, I have also created visual work for public TV broadcasts, "Brands", music organizations and music and video producers.



Projects

I like and consider the VJ side as a part of experimental Film. Experimental Film at "the Vrije Academie" (the Hague), was supervised by Frans Zwartjes in the 90s. During that period I received permission to use film equipment to start a film project together with the (Rietveld Academy) Digerido & visual artist Igor Ezendam. This ultimately resulted in 2 interactive (VJ) live performances.

The first in the Provincial house in The Hague as an interactive film/theater performance during the opening of the painting exhibition by artist Claudette Keuls.

The second during an interactive (VJ) performance/installation with Igor Ezendam in the Roxy for the opening of Thursday's theme evening "the Womb". Event producer Eelco Anquanois (Roxy, Chemistry, Marktkantine, a'dam).

Visuals


At the limits of immersive visual techniques. For me a painting, foto, a sea side or an image (looped) sequence are actually the same it triggers an intellectual, spiritual or emotional fascination or immersion. I am always searching in the (emmersive) contemporary technology to search for new forms.

Loops
My interests lie in the research of visual immersive techniques through our old and new contemporary knowledge and technology. Loops are essential elements in my VJ work. The recurring image loop sequences are necessary to arrive at a certain imagination or meaning.

The memorable visual signals are ultimately combined into an intellectual or emotional story to create a new and more powerful image sequence. In the image sequences, the story uses internal and external properties such as text, sound and rhythm to contribute to the associative story line and its intellectual or abstract context.
